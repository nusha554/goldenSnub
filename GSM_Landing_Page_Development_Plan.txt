================================================================

================
Transform the current React app into a stunning landing page for the GSM (Golden Snub Monkey) coin with countdown timer and scroll animations.

=============================

1. HERO SECTION WITH COUNTDOWN TIMER

   - Launch Countdown: Real-time countdown to October 25, 2025
   - Call-to-Action: "Join the Golden Revolution" button
   - Animated Background: Floating golden particles or subtle animations

2. SCROLL-TRIGGERED CONTENT SECTIONS
   - About GSM: What is Golden Snub Monkey coin
   - Tokenomics: Supply, distribution, utility
   - Roadmap: Development milestones and future plans
   - Team: Core team members and advisors
   - Partnerships: Strategic alliances and integrations
   - Community: Social links and community stats

3. INTERACTIVE ELEMENTS
   - Smooth Scrolling: Parallax effects and scroll animations
   - Hover Effects: Interactive cards and buttons
   - Loading Animations: Staggered text reveals
   - Mobile Optimization: Touch-friendly interactions
